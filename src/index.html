About us

Background History

The University of Computer Studies (Taunggyi) is situated in the Southern Shan State, Myanmar. The Government Computer College (Taunggyi) was established on August 15 in 2000. University of Computer Studies (Taunggyi) has been upgraded on 20th January 2007.

Location and Area

University of Computer Studies (Taunggyi) is situated on the west of the Union Road from Taunggyi to Hopone . It is four miles far from the Capital city, Taunggyi. The total area of the University of Computer (Taunggyi) has 215 acres.

Aims and Objectives

  • To produce qualified and outstanding ICT professionals for Shan state, Myanmar, Regions.
  • To give support to human resource development.
  • To have a chance to learn advanced technology without any gap between urban and state.
